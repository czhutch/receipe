Cheesy Egg Bake

Super Easy To Assemble and Bake!

Ingredients

  • 7 Eggs, Beaten
  • 1 Cup Almond Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Lite Sour Cream
  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheese of Choice
  • 1/3 Package Frozen Hashbrowns
  • Salt, Spices, and Pepper to Preference
  • Veggies &/or Meat of Choice

Instructions

Combine the beaten eggs, almond milk, and lite sour cream. After combining, fold in the cheese and hashrowns, veggies &/or meat. Add salt and pepper/spice to preference. Spray baking dish with cooking spray and fill with egg mixture. Add extra cheese to top. Bake at 375 degree oven for approx. 1 hour or until is cooked in center

>

Enjoy!
